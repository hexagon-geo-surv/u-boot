* Leica UART to the MCU

Required properties:

- leica,MCU-uart: marker for common/leica_sep to pickup the uart
  connected to an onboard MCU, for udevice initialisation and
  communication

- leica,MCU-uart-module: the $MODULE_NUMBER-1, needed to enable the
  corresponding clock

- leica,MCU-uart-baudrate: the baudrate to initialize the udevice
  driver with.


Additional requirements:

  the "leica,MCU-uart" marker should be placed only *once* in the
  devicetree, into the corresponding uartX node.

  Keep in mind to also configure the pull-ups on the uart lines
  "correctly" in the main devicetree.


Example:

	&uart4 {
		leica,MCU-uart;
		leica,MCU-uart-module = <3>;
		leica,MCU-uart-baudrate = <19200>;
	};
